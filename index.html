OUR TEAM

Meet the Visionaries Behind Kohinoor Film Festival

Our passionate leaders bring decades of combined experience in cinema, storytelling, and creative vision to make every film festival an unforgettable experience.

Dinkar Jadhav

DIRECTOR

Dinkar heads up our festival operations, bringing visionary leadership to Kohinoor Film Festival. A passionate cinephile and curator, he has dedicated his career to discovering emerging talent and celebrating diverse storytelling. His keen eye for exceptional cinema and commitment to artistic excellence has made him a respected figure in the film industry. When not busy curating films, Dinkar can be found exploring international film festivals, mentoring young filmmakers, or immersing himself in classic cinema.

Ganesh Gunjal

CO-DIRECTOR

Ganesh provides brilliant strategic support and creative direction to the festival. He's a meticulous planner who's all about the details and ensuring seamless execution. With a background in film production and festival management, Ganesh brings technical expertise and innovative thinking to every aspect of the festival. When he isn't busy organizing world-class film events, Ganesh enjoys cinematography, traveling to film locations, and collaborating with international filmmakers to bring diverse perspectives to the big screen.
